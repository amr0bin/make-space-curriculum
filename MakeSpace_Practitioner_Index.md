# Make Space — Practitioner Index

**HOW TO USE THIS INDEX**

Each entry gives you a name, field, and the work or body of practice most relevant to that week. These are real practitioners whose existence and general field I can attest to with reasonable confidence. However:

- **Specific book titles, years, journal articles, and quotes must be verified by you before use.** AI-generated citation details are a known source of plausible errors.
- **Session details, workshop outcomes, and practitioner statements not drawn from a published source must not be included in posts.**
- Entries marked **[VERIFY DETAILS]** are practitioners I am confident exist but whose specific works I am less certain about — verify before building into the template.
- Entries marked **[VERIFY EXISTENCE]** are practitioners I believe are real but where I have lower confidence — do not use without independent confirmation.

The index is a research starting point, not a finished reference list.
